⚡ SparkyTools — Residential Service Load Calculation

Date: SparkyTools v10 · NEC 2023 · Verify with a licensed electrician

Residential Service and Generator Sizing

Calculates required residential service size using the NEC Standard Method (220.40–220.53) or Optional Method (220.82/220.83). Each load row has two checkboxes: Shed excludes that load from a second calculation run, giving you a reduced service size for generator or ATS sizing; New marks the load as newly added, which is annotated in the 220.83 results for existing-dwelling upgrade documentation.
